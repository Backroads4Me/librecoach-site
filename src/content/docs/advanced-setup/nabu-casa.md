Nabu Casa is the official cloud service from the Home Assistant team. It provides easy remote access and voice assistant integration while supporting the continued development of Home Assistant.

## Features

- **Remote Access** - Access your Home Assistant from anywhere without port forwarding
- **Google Assistant Integration** - Control your RV with "Hey Google"
- **Amazon Alexa Integration** - Control your RV with Alexa
- **Support Home Assistant** - Subscription fees fund HA development

## Pricing

$6.50/month or $65/year (as of 2024)
