## Take Your RV Further

LibreCoach works with **any RV that uses the RV-C network**, from older rigs to brand-new models. Whether you want **simple control** or a platform to **expand with automations**, LibreCoach adapts to your needs.

Built on Home Assistant, it gives you an easy-to-use interface for lights, climate, tanks, and power systems — no prior experience required. Advanced users can dive deeper, customize dashboards, or integrate additional devices at their own pace.

### Why LibreCoach?

- **Works With Any RV-C RV** – Supports decades of RVs from all manufacturers.
- **Open Source & Community-Driven** – Fully transparent, auditable, and extensible. Contributions help the project grow.
- **Flexible for Everyone** – Basic control works immediately, while advanced users can customize and extend their system.
- **Reliable & Future-Proof** – Add automations, integrate devices, and keep evolving without vendor lock-in.

## What Is LibreCoach?

LibreCoach bridges your RV’s **RV-C network** with **Home Assistant**, providing:

- Simple, ready-to-use controls for everyday RV operations
- Optional advanced automations and customizations for power users
- A system that evolves with your rig and your ideas
