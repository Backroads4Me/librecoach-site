**Last Updated:** January 28, 2026

Your motorhome's data belongs to you — not a cloud provider.

## Philosophy

- **Local-First:** LibreCoach runs locally on your hardware. Your RV data stays inside your RV.
- **No Tracking:** LibreCoach does not track your location, usage habits, or system data.
- **Open Transparency:** The code is open-source — you can verify exactly how your data is handled at any time.

## Data Collection

Data collection is intentionally limited to the absolute minimum:

### 1. Interest Checks & Waitlists

If you sign up for updates regarding the **Pre-Assembled Kit**, your **email address** is collected.

- **Usage:** Strictly to notify you if/when the hardware becomes available.
- **No Sharing:** Email addresses are never sold, traded, or shared with third parties.

### 2. Website Analytics

This documentation site may collect anonymous, aggregate usage data (such as page views) to understand which guides are most useful. This data does not contain personally identifiable information.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/backroads4me).
