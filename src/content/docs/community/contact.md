The best way to get in touch is through our community spaces, where your questions can help others building similar systems.

### Technical Support & Discussion

For questions about building your own LibreCoach, hardware compatibility, or troubleshooting, please join us on the **[LibreCoach Community Forum](https://forum.librecoach.com)**.

### Bug Reports & Contributions

If you've found a technical bug or want to contribute to the code, please open an issue or pull request on our **[GitHub Repository](https://github.com/Backroads4Me)**.

### General & Private Inquiries

For security disclosures, administrative matters, or other private inquiries, please contact us at **hello@librecoach.com**.
